02Scouting Report

Usyk built his résumé at cruiserweight as the most complete boxer of his generation — footwork, angles, ring IQ — before moving up to heavyweight and beating three of the division's biggest names using the exact same toolkit, largely unchanged.

Style

Southpaw, high work rate, constantly changes angles to neutralize bigger, heavier-handed opponents. Rarely stationary long enough to be hit clean.

X-Factor

Conditioning. Usyk's pace in championship rounds has repeatedly been the deciding factor against fighters who looked competitive through six.
